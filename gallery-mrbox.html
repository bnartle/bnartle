How it came to be

I had great fun collaborating with author Szabolcs Bogyor on his children's picture book about Vienna. It's been an absolute joy to work alongside someone with so many fun ideas, and I'm not only speaking of his writing. It's a wonder to see these sketches (below) from author Szabolcs Bogyor as we explored ideas for illustrations, which inspired me greatly. I'm honestly amazed!
