About

Hi! I'm Trân, or Jun, an illustrator from Vietnam. I graduated with a BFA in Graphic Design from HCMC University of Architecture, with distinct biannual scholarships. My education provides me with much needed fundamentals as an artist.

I'm most fond of children's book illustration, with a love for a gentle, organic and natural style. I find joy in telling stories beyond the words and giving life to my creation through expressive colors, movements and expressions. I love painting scenes with many characters, with each having their own thing to do and story to tell. They all come together to make the scene so bustling and alive. When I was younger, I thought I would really love to be a performer on stage. I'm not one, yet, but at least with illustrating, I can show a lot of faces when no one is watching.
