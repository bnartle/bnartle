The Forest

Inspired by Margaret Mahy's The Good Wizard of Forest, this book tells a heart-warming story of a lonely man who adopted a seedling tree and ended up planting a forest by accident.

See other projects

How it came to be

This children's picture book is part of my graduation project from university, a personal adpatation of the story a short story from New Zealander author Margaret Mahy. You can find the original story here. The project is featured on Behance's Photoshop and Illustration tabs; and designideas.pics.

Tree were company for trees. People were company for people. Tree were company for people and people were company for trees. [...] Nobody was lonely. [...] So that was all right.

The good wizard of forest

Margaret Mahy
